Common Questions

Got Questions?

No app download is needed. The ordering experience is fully web-based — students access it from their phone browser, scan a QR code, or follow a link. Nothing to install, no friction at the point of order.

We're currently onboarding pilot canteens at no cost to refine the platform. Pricing for full rollout will be announced after the pilot phase — early adopters receive preferential rates.

Most canteens are fully live within 24–48 hours. We handle onboarding — menu upload, admin setup, and staff walkthrough. No technical knowledge required.

The platform is cloud-based and requires internet. A basic mobile data connection is sufficient — no broadband or special hardware needed. A simple Wi-Fi hotspot works as backup.

The platform supports prepaid orders — payment is collected at the point of ordering, not pickup. This eliminates no-shows and guarantees you never prepare food for uncollected orders.

Why We Built This

We Saw Canteens
Bleeding Revenue

Every day, campus canteens were preparing food blindly — no idea how many students would arrive, what they'd order, or when demand would peak. The result: unsold food wasted, students left hungry, and lost sales the canteen never knew it could have had.

Long queues at peak hours, food shortages during rush, overproduction during slow periods, inventory managed through guesswork. Canteen owners were working harder than ever but operating in the dark.

We asked: what if the kitchen knew exactly what to prepare before the day even started? That question became Campus Crawiiings — built not for students, but for the people running the canteen.

"Manage demand
before it arrives.
Profit from every
order."

The Platform

Built for
Operational Excellence

Campus Crawiiings connects students directly to your kitchen. They pre-order through the platform — you receive those orders in real time with precise quantities, item breakdowns, and pickup timing.

Your staff no longer guesses how much to cook. They prepare exactly what's been ordered. Zero guesswork. Zero waste. Maximum efficiency. Every shift starts with a clear picture of the day ahead.

As orders come in, your analytics dashboard tracks demand patterns, revenue, peak times, and best-performing items. Over time, you're not just running a canteen — you're running a smart food operation backed by real data.
